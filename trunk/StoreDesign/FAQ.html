Frequently Asked Questions

What are the decals made of?

They are made from none-toxic and quality PVC.

How will the decal be packed and mailed?

We would like to 100% meet our customer satisfaction, so the decal will be put either into outer-mailing tube or wrapped by satchel with a linear tube inside.

Are those decals easy to apply?

Yes. Some decals do not have transferring tape, so just peel off backing paper and stick, installation of other decals with applied transferring tape could be easily done by following steps on instruction sheet which is included in product delivery.

Will the decal ruin my wall?

Theoretically,no. However pre-test is highly recommended. Using a piece of masking tape and place it on the wall,if the paint remains on the wall and not on the tape as you lift it up, then your wall is most likely be suitable for wall decals.

Are those decals removeable or even reusable?

Yes, our decals are removeable for sure, while with reusability, it really depends on the surface. If the surface is smooth and flat, the answer is yes, otherwise probably not.

How long will the decal last?

Our decals could last around 5 years or even longer in standard interior environment.

Can I pick another colour or choose different size to suit my space?

Yes, let us know what colour and size you like, then we could make the order for you. Please keep in mind that customer order will be shipped from our overseas manufacturer, so delivery will take longer than usual, and also price might be more.

Do you wholesale decals?

Yes, email us quantity and description of products that you like, we will send you free quote in 24 hours.
